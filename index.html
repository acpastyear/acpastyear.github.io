About This Webpage

This is a free webpage created by a UTP AC senior to help the future juniors in their studies in Applied Chemistry (AC) course in UTP.

Sharing is caring. However, please do not reveal this to any authorities (including GAs, lecturers, Head of Departments etc.) I do not know how legal this website is XD.
